During the entirety of the exam, it is forbidden to have within reach, in the exam room, or next to the exam room any material that is related to the exam itself or the subject of the exam, except for material that has been permitted by the instructor. If a calculator is needed for the exam, only simple scientific calculators that have no means of communication can be used.
