Students who are not around during the appeal period due to reserve duty, will receive an extension for the date by which the appeal must be filed in accordance with the number of days that they were in reserve duty.
Assignments Students who are absent due to reserve duty in the two weeks prior to the submission deadline of an assignment will receive an extension for at least the number of days that they were in reserve duty. In the case that the new submission date falls during the exam period, the deadline will be postponed until after the exam period.
